# POKĂMON CHAMPIONS - WEATHER EFFECTS & MECHANICS
==================================================
This document defines the active modifiers, stat boosts, and damage scalings triggered by the four core weather conditions in the VGC format.

## 1. SUN (HARSH SUNLIGHT / SUNNY DAY)
*   **Damage Modifiers:** 
    *   Increases the power of `Fire`-type moves by **1.5x**.
    *   Decreases the power of `Water`-type moves by **0.5x**.
*   **Mechanics & Stat Boosts:**
    *   Prevents PokĂŠmon from being frozen (`Freeze`).
    *   Activates the `Protosynthesis` ability (boosts the highest stat by 1.3x, or 1.5x if it is Speed).
    *   Activates the `Chlorophyll` ability (doubles the PokĂŠmon's actual Speed stat).
    *   `Thunder` and `Hurricane` accuracy is reduced to **50%**.
    *   `Solar Beam` and `Solar Blade` bypass the charging turn (hits instantly).

## 2. RAIN (RAIN DANCE)
*   **Damage Modifiers:**
    *   Increases the power of `Water`-type moves by **1.5x**.
    *   Decreases the power of `Fire`-type moves by **0.5x**.
*   **Mechanics & Stat Boosts:**
    *   Activates the `Swift Swim` ability (doubles the PokĂŠmon's actual Speed stat).
    *   `Thunder` and `Hurricane` bypass accuracy checks (always hits, bypasses accuracy/evasion stages).
    *   Reduces the healing from `Morning Sun`, `Synthesis`, and `Moonlight` to **25%** of max HP.

## 3. SANDSTORM (SANDSTREAM)
*   **Damage Modifiers:**
    *   At the end of each turn, damages all non-`Rock`, non-`Ground`, and non-`Steel` PokĂŠmon by **1/16th (6.25%)** of their maximum HP.
*   **Mechanics & Stat Boosts:**
    *   Increases the **Special Defense (SpD)** of all `Rock`-type PokĂŠmon by **1.5x**.
    *   Activates the `Sand Rush` ability (doubles the PokĂŠmon's actual Speed stat).
    *   Reduces the healing from `Morning Sun`, `Synthesis`, and `Moonlight` to **25%** of max HP.

## 4. SNOW (SNOWSCAPE)
*   **Damage Modifiers:**
    *   No direct damage scaling to moves.
*   **Mechanics & Stat Boosts:**
    *   Increases the **Defense (Def)** of all `Ice`-type PokĂŠmon by **1.5x**.
    *   Activates the `Slush Rush` ability (doubles the PokĂŠmon's actual Speed stat).
    *   `Blizzard` bypasses accuracy checks (always hits, bypasses accuracy/evasion stages).
    *   Reduces the healing from `Morning Sun`, `Synthesis`, and `Moonlight` to **25%** of max HP.
